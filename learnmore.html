Documenting my processes for Tech Enthusiast

This platform is dedicated to providing "easy-to-follow-along" tech tutorial and documentations. I will publish my processes as I attempt to walk through seemingly challenging tasks and hopefully that will give some insight if you are in similar situations or faced with similar challenges.
Topics will range from networking to virtualization, devops, containers, linux systems, cyber security and datacenter technologies.

I started out actively in tech as a Cisco Networking Academy instructor and quickly transitioned into Systems Engineering as I became responsible for a cluster of servers; implementation of virtualization technologies; creation, configuration and patching of VMs; hyperconverged infrastructure and implementation of DataCenter best practices. Being aware of how difficult it could get sometimes to find online resources to guide one through certain quick task (and I have been there a lot), I figured it'd be a great idea to document my processes going forward and publish them to help anyone who needs help at any particular time.
